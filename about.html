In 2013, we laid the foundation for an “Online institute”, and since then, our fields of work have diversified and expanded, leading to the formation of “Yousry Group”.

Today, our group operates in multiple countries and boasts a team of over 2000 employees, including both full-time and contract workers. Our mission remains the same – to expand our presence in new fields, open more branches in different countries, and create job opportunities for those who share our vision and possess the capabilities to make it a reality.

At Yousry Group, we specialize in several fields, including education and training, general trading and contracting, software development, website design, and digital marketing. Our diverse portfolio enables us to serve clients across various industries, ranging from health clubs to advertising agencies.

We are dedicated to providing exceptional services to our clients, and are constantly striving to improve and innovate our offerings. Our team is comprised of highly skilled professionals with extensive experience in their respective fields, and we are committed to creating a culture of growth and learning within our organization.

We are excited about the future and look forward to continuing to expand our presence globally while delivering high-quality services to our clients.
